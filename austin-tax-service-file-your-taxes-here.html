Frequently Asked Questions

How do CPAs stay updated with changes in tax laws and regulations?

CPAs stay updated with changes in tax laws and regulations through continuing education, professional development programs, and membership in professional organizations. It's crucial for CPAs to stay informed to provide accurate and timely advice to their clients.

When should I consider hiring a CPA for my business?

You should consider hiring a CPA for your business when you have complex financial situations, need assistance with tax planning, face an audit from the IRS, or require ongoing financial advice. CPAs can provide valuable insights and expertise in various financial matters.

Can a CPA provide remote consultations and services?

Yes, many CPAs offer remote consultations and services, leveraging technology to communicate with clients and handle financial matters online. This flexibility allows individuals and businesses to access CPA expertise without the need for in-person meetings.
